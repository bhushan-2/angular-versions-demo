Angular 10 has come with the all-new date range picker. To use it, you can utilize elements of both mat date range picker and mat date range input. If you want your users to select a range of dates, instead of a single date, you can use the mat-date-range-input and mat-date-range-picker components. They work in tandem, similarly to the mat-datepicker and the basic datepicker input.
